Zadra

Zadra is the brand that my sister and I are creating together hence the brand name. We are still in an early trial and error stage at the moment of pattern making and toiling, seeing what prints are popular and what aren’t.

We have made some beautiful samples including a bomber jacket, raglan top and a sundress as well as accessories like headbands and home décor like cushion covers.

Next we are trialling more home décor items such as: reusable beeswax lunch wraps, tea towels, cushions and baby items such as play mats and swaddles.

Crookednature

Crooked Nature is my little eco baby, something I have been refining for a while now. Crooked Nature is my Natural dyeing and eco printing brand!

I first started making just naturally dyed kimono’s using just flowers and getting a beautiful splotchy colour dye and then slowly got better and did more research into using leaves to print onto the fabric.

I went on from kimonos to, silk scarves, woollen scarves, headbands, long sleeve tops and baby jumpsuits.

You can get some amazing colours and effects just from using different natural combinations. I’m excited to expand my skills and methods and create more complex garments.
