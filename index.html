I make beautiful things for beautiful people.

I am Francesca Zadra, 28 and lover of all things fashion.

I have been sewing since I was 4 years old when my mum taught me how to sew buttons onto a piece of scrap fabric which I still have today. By the time I was 17 I was making outfits for myself, like tops, shorts, dresses etc.

Being called ‘fashion girl’ during high school and getting teased when I tried to wear jeans and Ugg boots like everyone else at my school, I went on to study a Diploma of Fashion Design and Technology at RMIT in Brunswick Victoria.

Style

My style is generally a bit quirky and fun but it can also depend on my mood, what I’m making for what occasion. My inspiration can come from anything and everything, a fabric that I like or a print, nature or a scenery or a decade in time, my inspiration comes from all over.

Influences

Alice McCall, Zimmerman, Alannah Hill, Aje, Lisa Gorman, Keepsake.

The Classics - Channel, Dior, Yves Saint Laurent.

Bridal - Vivienne Westwood, Marianna Hardwick, Grace Loves Lace.

Goals

I have heard that if you love your job then you never really work a day in your life, this is what I hope to someday achieve. I really enjoy what I do and I love when what I have created makes someone happy, so to do this and see someone walking down the street in something I have designed would be goal achieved for me.
